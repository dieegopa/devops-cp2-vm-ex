Elden Ring Features

Vast Open World

Explore the Lands Between, a new fantasy world created by Hidetaka Miyazaki and George R.R. Martin. Traverse the breathtaking world on foot or on horseback, alone or online with other players, and fully immerse yourself in the grassy plains, suffocating swamps, spiraling mountains, and foreboding castles.

Uncover the mysteries of the Elden Ring's power and become the Elden Lord!

Epic Battles and Fearsome Enemies

Face incredibly challenging foes and terrifying creatures in Elden Ring's intense combat system. From common enemies to epic bosses, each battle will test your skills and strategy.

Customize your character's playstyle with a wide variety of weapons, magical abilities, and skills to overcome the game's many challenges.

Rich Lore and Storytelling

Immerse yourself in a deep, dark fantasy universe created by Hidetaka Miyazaki, creator of the influential Dark Souls video game series, and George R.R. Martin, author of The New York Times best-selling fantasy series, A Song of Ice and Fire.

Unravel the mysteries of the Lands Between, discover hidden truths, and shape the world's fate through your choices and actions.

Freedom of Exploration and Customization

Elden Ring offers unparalleled freedom in how you approach your adventure. Create your own unique character, define your playstyle, and decide how to explore the vast world before you.

Experiment with different weapons, armor, magic, and skills to find the perfect combination that suits your preferred way of playing.

Ready to Begin Your Journey?

Embark on an epic adventure in the Lands Between and become the Elden Lord!
